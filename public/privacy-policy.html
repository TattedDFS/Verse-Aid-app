Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

VerseAid ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the VerseAid application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account and use VerseAid:

  • Account information: email address and username you provide at registration
  • Prayer journal entries: personal prayer journal content you write within the app
  • Saved responses: AI-generated Biblical guidance you choose to save
  • Reading progress: your progress through Bible reading plans
  • Community prayers: prayer requests you voluntarily share to the community prayer wall
  • Questions submitted: questions you ask the AI guidance feature
  • Subscription status: your current plan tier (free or premium)

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, camera access, or any data beyond what is described above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain your VerseAid account and personalized experience
  • Deliver AI-powered Biblical guidance responses through Anthropic's Claude AI
  • Store and sync your prayer journal, saved responses, and reading progress across devices
  • Process subscription payments and manage your account tier
  • Respond to support inquiries sent to our contact email
  • Improve the app based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase
We use Supabase for user authentication and secure data storage. Your account credentials and app data are stored on Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase processes data in accordance with their privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

Anthropic (Claude AI)
Questions you submit to the Biblical guidance feature are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Anthropic may process these inputs in accordance with their usage policies. We do not send personally identifying information (such as your name or email) alongside your questions.

RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases on iOS. RevenueCat processes purchase receipts and subscription status on our behalf. Payment card information is handled entirely by Apple and is never accessible to VerseAid or RevenueCat directly.

4. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. We share your data only with the third-party service providers listed above, and only to the extent necessary to operate the app.

5. Community Prayer Wall

If you choose to share a prayer request to the community prayer wall, that content becomes visible to other registered VerseAid users. You share this content voluntarily. Do not post personally sensitive information on the community prayer wall that you would not want other users to see.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data (journal entries, saved responses, account credentials) is permanently deleted from our systems. Community prayer posts may remain visible for a short period while caches clear.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication through Supabase. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

VerseAid is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at verseaid.ai@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (via account deletion in the app)
  • Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at verseaid.ai@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of VerseAid after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
